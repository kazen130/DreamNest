About This Website

This website was created especially for Mizo users who love watching animated movies in Mizo language. Our goal is to make it simple, small, and easy to use — even if your connection is slow or your device is not powerful.

We're using a free host (Aimeo), so the website may not look perfect or fast, and the video quality might not be the best right now. We sincerely apologize for that.

But we're working hard to improve everything — better design, better quality, and more Mizo version movies and series are coming soon!

Thank you so much for visiting and supporting. We hope you enjoy your time here.
